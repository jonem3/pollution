The data on this website is being retrieved from Met Office Datapoint and London Air by Imperial College London. The data on this page is being generated by a Tensorflow model, which is a Python package for generating algorithms to predict data values, that was the main purpose of this EPQ. The model is being trained on an assortment of hourly weather readings that have been recorded since February 2020 and matched up pollution data where the site is within 15 miles of the weather station. The scale of the pollution indexes goes from 1 - 10. This is explained well by the following table that can be found on the Department for Environment Food & Rural Affairs website
